Organizer

Evan is currently a freshman at High Technology High School in Lincroft, New Jersey. He founded OMMC with Ishaan Akula in September of 2020 and has worked as a problem creator for the contest. He has participated in numerous math competitions over the years, including MATHCOUNTS, AMC 10/12, AIME, and USAMTS. He has also been involved with the Mathily series of summer programs. He has participated in the WOOT program on AoPS and enjoys communicating with others on the online forums, you can find him there as squareman. Outside of mathematics, he enjoys composing music, having won music composition competitions at the state level. In his free time, he enjoys solving puzzles and going on long, relaxing walks. He also eats way too much food.
