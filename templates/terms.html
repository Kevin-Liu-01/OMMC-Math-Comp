CODE OF CONDUCT

As a math competition, we have a commitment to uphold the highest standards of academic integrity, and we expect all competitors to do the same. Cheating in this contest (refer to the competition rules) is extremely frowned upon - the main purpose of OMMC is to foster interest in mathematics and aid in mathematical development, and cheating on this contest helps with none of these things.

TERMS OF SERVICE

All participants in the OMMC event are required to read and understand all rules outlined in the Official OMMC Rulebook. To access the rulebook, click here. Any form of inappropriate behavior is prohibited, and OMMC reserves the right to ban or prohibit participation in this competition if misconduct is found. All of the problems in the competition are the intellectual property of OMMC, and any instances of plagiarism or theft of OMMC problems may be subject to consequences.

CONTACT US

We recommend that the majority of questions surrounding the competition be sent to the Official OMMC email. Send an email to one of the administrators only in the event that you want to address one of them individually. If you have witnessed or experienced any violations of the OMMC Code of Conduct event rules, please contact us immediately.
